About Axis ELSS Tax Saver Direct Plan Growth
Axis ELSS Tax Saver Direct Plan Growth is a Equity Mutual Fund Scheme launched by Axis Mutual Fund. This scheme was made available to investors on 04 Sep 2009. Shreyash Devalkar is the Current Fund Manager of Axis ELSS Tax Saver Direct Plan Growth fund. The fund currently has an Asset Under Management(AUM) of ₹3,58,698 Cr and the Latest NAV as of 02 Mar 2026 is ₹107.52. The Axis ELSS Tax Saver Direct Plan Growth is rated Very High risk. Minimum SIP Investment is set to ₹500. Minimum Lumpsum Investment is ₹500.

The scheme aims to generate regular long term capital growth from a diversified portfolio of equity and equity related securities. The Scheme Will invest in companies with strong growth & a sustainable business model.
