About Axis Liquid Direct Fund Growth
Axis Liquid Direct Fund Growth is a Debt Mutual Fund Scheme launched by Axis Mutual Fund. This scheme was made available to investors on 04 Sep 2009. Devang Shah is the Current Fund Manager of Axis Liquid Direct Fund Growth fund. The fund currently has an Asset Under Management(AUM) of ₹3,58,698 Cr and the Latest NAV as of 02 Mar 2026 is ₹3,049.83. The Axis Liquid Direct Fund Growth is rated Low to Moderate risk. Minimum SIP Investment is set to ₹100. Minimum Lumpsum Investment is ₹100.
